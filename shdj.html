ABOUT US

Our Story

SkyHigh Balloons was founded in 2018 with a passion for transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences through the art of balloon decoration.

What started as a small home business has grown into one of the most sought-after balloon decor services in the region, thanks to our commitment to quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction.

Our team of certified balloon artists brings years of experience and fresh ideas to every event, ensuring your decor is unique, on-trend, and perfectly suited to your vision.

We use only the highest quality balloons and materials, and our installations are designed to last throughout your entire event.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my balloon decor?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for smaller events and 4-6 weeks for weddings or large events. During peak seasons, even earlier booking is advisable.

How long do balloon decorations last?

Our high-quality latex balloons typically last 12-24 hours indoors, while foil balloons can last several days. Outdoor installations are weather-dependent but usually last 6-12 hours.

Do you offer custom designs?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom designs tailored to your event theme, color scheme, and personal style. Bring us your ideas and we'll bring them to life!
